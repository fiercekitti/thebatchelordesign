Getting Started:

Step 1: Enroll to Sell the H-DTM Gift Card Program

This is the first critical document you will need to complete! Please complete this agreement and send to: hddealersupport@storefinancial.com or fax to 866-896-1674.

  • H-DTM Canada Gift Card Selling Location Agreement (English) (French)

Step 2: EFT Debit Authorization Form (English) (French)

To sell gift cards, you must designate a bank account for Store Financial to pull the gift card activation amount, activation fee and applicable taxes from. You can establish a dedicated bank account or use an existing account if desired. We recommend you put a beginning balance into the account for protection. The timing of EFT transactions will be covered in training.

Please complete the form and return it, along with the required void check/bank letter, to hddealersupport@storefinancial.com or fax to 866-896-1674.

Step 3: User Request Form (English) (French)

Each employee that you designate to selling cards or running reports will need their own individual “user account”. Follow the instructions on the User Request Form. Return the completed form to hddealersupport@storefinancial.com or fax to 866-896-1674.

Step 4: Enroll to Redeem (accept) the new H-DTM Gift Card

After you have returned the executed H-DTM Canada Gift Card Selling Location Agreement, you will receive 2 emails. Each email will contain necessary information to help us set you up as an “authorized redeeming location”. Each email will contain a portion of a virtual gift card number that you will use to complete a test “sales” transaction.

  • H-DTM Canada Gift Card Redeeming Dealer Agreement (English) (French)
    Print out your redeeming dealer agreement and keep it with you when you complete the test transaction. You will need to record information.

The two test transaction emails will come from hddealersupport@storefinancial.com. If you have not received the emails within three business days after submitting the Selling Location Agreement, please check your spam folder before contacting hddealersupport@storefinancial.com.

  1. Locate the virtual gift card number sent in the two separate emails.
  2. Perform a sales transaction to start the process. Ring up any item for any amount (it will be declined).
  3. When you are asked for payment, enter the virtual gift card number information just as you would with any other credit card that needs to be keyed in manually.
  4. The card will decline because it is inactive and has no funds.
  5. Record all the test transaction information in the fields provided on the printed Redeeming Dealer Agreement.
  6. Review the remainder of the agreement and sign to execute the agreement.
  7. Return the completed form to: hddealersupport@storefinancial.com or fax to 866-896-1674.

  • Merchant Redemption Reference Guide (English) (French)
    Review the Merchant Redemption Reference document for tips on accepting the card.



H-DTM Gift Card Reference Material and Program Information

  1. Software Install

    This program uses “CardSpot Gift Card Activation” Software (Prior to installation, you must receive the credentials requested in step 4 to be able to configure the software.)

    • Minimum System requirements: The CardSpot Gift Card Activation Software is PC based (Windows Vista or Windows 7 only), requiring Microsoft.Net Framework version 3.5 Service Pack 1 (SP1) and High Speed internet access. A local network printer or a dedicated USB receipt printer is required for activation receipts. Microsoft Internet Explorer version 8.0 (or higher) or Mozilla Firefox version 4.0 (or higher) is required for reporting access. Please see the H-DTM CAN System Requirements for CardSpot (English) (French) for further details.
    • Review the CardSpot Installation Guide (English) (French).
    • Please note there is a generic username/password contained in the Installation Guide that will be used when downloading the software. After the download, the username/password received after submitting the User Request Form (Step 1.4) will be used to configure and login to CardSpot.
    • If you need assistance downloading or configuring the software please call 800-803-7537.

  2. Training

    Each dealership that wishes to sell or accept the new H-DTM Gift Card will be required to complete training before they will be activated in the program. Once we have a record that training has been completed, you will be turned on to sell and redeem cards. Live training dates will be provided followed by a recorded webinar that your dealership employees can attend.

    • Attend a live webinar or view a recorded webinar training session (Training is 1.5 - 2 hours).
      • Live webinar schedule (English & French)
      • Recorded webinar training session:
        • English
        • French
    • Review the CardSpot Quick Reference Guide (English) (French) - this guide outlines the entire H-DTM Gift Card selling process.
    • Review and understand the Suggested Reconciliation Procedures (English) (French) – This is a suggested procedure and optional form. It is recommended that the H-DTM Gift Card reconciliation be incorporated into the same process you use for closing out a register and reconciling.
    • Review and understand the Operating Regulations (English) (French).
    • Review the H-DTM Canada Gift Card Support document (English) (French).
