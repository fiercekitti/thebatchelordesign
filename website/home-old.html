Web Design

AirFixture
AirFixture Description

AirFixture is a local Kansas City company that had come to my employer for marketing help. First on the agenda was a new website design and CMS system. After hearing their troubles with Joomla, I suggested they migrate to WordPress. With the assistance of one of the graphic designers, we came up with a WordPress theme that worked for AirFixture and I got the building. I included some custom coding to give them the perfect look, built out all their main pages, and made suggestions for photo and page layouts. They have recently launched their new website and are loving the new design and the ease their staff can make changes to it. I also provided training on how to use WordPress and how to make changes.

Enrollment Portal Description
